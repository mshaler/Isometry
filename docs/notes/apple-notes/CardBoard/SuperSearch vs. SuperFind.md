SuperSearch vs. SuperFind

* SuperSearch is BFS faceted database search in the manner of HotNotes.app
* SuperFind is find/replace for current SuperGrid
* SuperTimeSlicer is time series data parser on ingest
* SuperParent/SuperChild are the components of SuperCards (stacked parent headers and embedded child filter nav cards all/Q1/Q2/etc/none enabling non-contiguous selection) and interact with SuperSelect
