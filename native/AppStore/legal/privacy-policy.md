# Privacy Policy - Isometry

**Effective Date:** January 27, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

## Overview

Isometry ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Isometry application (the "Service").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect the following personal information:
- Name and email address (for account creation and sync)
- Device identifiers (for CloudKit synchronization)
- Usage analytics (anonymized for app improvement)

### Content Data
- Notes, documents, and other content you create or import
- Metadata associated with your content (creation dates, modification times)
- Graph connections and relationships you establish between content

### Technical Data
- Device type, operating system version
- App version and feature usage patterns
- Performance metrics and crash reports (anonymized)
- Network connectivity information for sync operations

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Synchronization**: Enable cross-device sync via CloudKit
- **Functionality**: Provide core app features and intelligent connections
- **Performance**: Optimize app performance and user experience
- **Support**: Provide customer support and troubleshooting

### Analytics and Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
- Monitor performance metrics to ensure optimal user experience
- Generate anonymized insights for feature development

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All content is stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite databases
- Local data is protected by iOS/macOS system-level encryption
- No content is transmitted to our servers except through CloudKit

### CloudKit Integration
- We use Apple's CloudKit service for cross-device synchronization
- CloudKit data is end-to-end encrypted and controlled by Apple
- We cannot access your CloudKit data; only you can access it with your Apple ID

### Security Measures
- Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
- Secure communication protocols (HTTPS, TLS 1.3)
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- No third-party data sharing except as required by law

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Third-Party Services
We integrate with the following third-party services:
- **Apple CloudKit**: For cross-device synchronization (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
- **Apple Analytics**: For anonymized usage metrics (can be disabled in Settings)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
- Protect the rights, property, or safety of Isometry, our users, or others
- Investigate fraud, security issues, or technical problems

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
- View and export all your data through the app's export functionality
- Delete your content at any time (local deletion is immediate)
- Control CloudKit sync through your Apple ID settings

### Analytics Opt-Out
- Disable analytics collection in app Settings
- Turn off crash reporting in iOS/macOS System Preferences
- Manage CloudKit data through your Apple ID account settings

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)
If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:
- Right of access to your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

## Children's Privacy

Isometry is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in various countries where Apple operates CloudKit infrastructure. Apple ensures appropriate safeguards for international data transfers in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by:
- Posting the updated policy in the app
- Sending notifications through the app interface
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Data Retention

- Content data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
- Usage analytics: Retained for 24 months in anonymized form
- Crash reports: Retained for 12 months for debugging purposes
- Account information: Retained while your account is active

## Technical Implementation

### Encryption Standards
- AES-256 encryption for local data storage
- TLS 1.3 for all network communications
- CloudKit end-to-end encryption for synchronized data

### Data Minimization
We collect only the data necessary to provide Service functionality:
- No location data collection
- No contact list access
- No unnecessary permissions requested
- Minimal analytics collection focused on performance

### Open Source Components
This app uses open source libraries with their own privacy implications:
- GRDB.swift (database management)
- Swift standard libraries (Apple's privacy policy applies)

This Privacy Policy is designed to be transparent about our data practices while ensuring your privacy and security remain our top priorities.
