## Goal

Remove the redundant ViewZipper horizontal tab strip and make SidebarNav's Visualization Explorer section the single source of truth for view switching. Preserve crossfade transitions, keyboard shortcuts (Cmd+1–9), and screen reader announcements.

## Requirements

- **NAV-01**: ViewZipper.ts and view-zipper.css are deleted; no import references remain
- **NAV-02**: SidebarNav Visualization Explorer section defaults to `data-state="visible"` (expanded)
- **NAV-03**: Crossfade transition (opacity 0→1) preserved on all view switches
- **NAV-04**: Cmd+1–9 shortcuts and CommandRegistry entries work without ViewZipper
- **NAV-05**: `window.__isometry` no longer exposes `viewZipper`
